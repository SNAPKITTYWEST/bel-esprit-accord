03 · THE CREDIT SEQUENCE

Eight Phases to Institutional Trust

Corporate credit is not fake. It is earned in sequence. Each phase builds on the last. You cannot skip a phase. Boring is bankable. Chaos is expensive.

PHASE 01
IDENTITY
EIN · Articles · Operating agreement · Business address · Domain · Website. Consistent everywhere institutions look.
PHASE 02
BANKING
Major FDIC first. Clean behavior. Consistent deposits. No overdrafts. No commingling. No mystery transfers.
PHASE 03
PAYMENT RAILS
Stripe in production with real compliance. Plaid connected for cash flow verification. Clean payment history.
PHASE 04
ACCOUNTING MEMORY
Clean books. Without books the enterprise has no memory. No memory means no credit.
PHASE 05
VENDOR CREDIT
Pay early. Low utilization. Do not over-apply. Payment history is the first objective. Not debt.
PHASE 06
SOFTWARE PROCUREMENT
Rippling, NetSuite, Salesforce extend terms to serious entities. Procurement credit is real capital when documented correctly.
PHASE 07
TREASURY
Fidelity or Schwab relationship. Investment policy. Asset custody. Demonstrates treasury discipline.
PHASE 08
TRUST INTERLOCK
IP assigned to trust. License documented. Operating agreement in place. No mystery movement between entities.
04 · THE TECHNOLOGY STACK

Why Every Technology Was Chosen

Every component in the stack was selected for a specific reason. Not trend. Not convenience. Institutional necessity.

COBOL
Financial institutions still run on old steel. If your stack cannot talk to legacy rails it cannot talk to banks.
OCR
Not document automation. Credit infrastructure. Every receipt becomes structured evidence. Every invoice becomes underwriting data.
STRIPE + PLAID
BaaS layer. Stripe = payment signal. Plaid = cash flow signal. Together they create the financial nervous system.
WORM CHAIN
Immutable audit trail. Ed25519 signed. SHA-256 sealed. Every action receipted. No mystery movement. Ever.
PROLOG KERNEL
Constraint enforcement before execution. No action advances unless every previous state remains coherent.
DASHBOARD
Without visibility the enterprise is blind. The dashboard is not a feature. It is institutional sight.
